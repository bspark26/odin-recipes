Breakfast Bagel

Description

This is basiaclly my every morning breakfast. Fast, easy, delicious!


This is a very basic recipe. Only requires a few basic ingredients that most people should have on hand.


You can make this in two ways:

  • Open faced
  • Closed "sandwich style"


Ingredients

  • 1 or 2 eggs.
  • Slices or cheese (I like sharp cheddar).
  • Butter or oil (for pan).
  • Salt to taste.
  • Pepper to taste.
  1. Toast bagel(s), add a bit of oil to pan and start cooking eggs on medium low heat.
  2. Cut cheese, butter bagel once done toasting.
  3. Finish egg to desired yokiness.
  4. Assemble bage, cheese on bagel, egg on top of cheese. Enjoy!
